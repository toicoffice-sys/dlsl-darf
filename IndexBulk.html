⚠️Conditions of Access

By submitting this request, the Data Recipient(s) acknowledge and agree to the following conditions:

  • Only the Data Recipients named in this form are authorized to access and use the personal data.
  • Access to the personal data is limited to the duration specified in this form. Any extension shall require the prior approval of the Data Privacy Office.
  • The personal data shall be used solely for the declared and authorized purpose(s).
  • Data Recipients are expected to implement appropriate security measures to preserve the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of the personal data.
  • The Data Recipient(s) shall not download, reproduce, or otherwise copy the file containing the personal data, whether in printed or electronic form, without the prior approval of the Data Privacy Office.
  • Upon the expiration of the approved access period, the Data Recipient(s) shall cease further processing of the personal data and securely dispose of such data in their custody. Upon request, a written certification of data deletion or disposal shall be submitted to the Data Owner and/or Processor, with a copy furnished to the Data Privacy Office.
